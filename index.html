How It Works
🦕
DINOv2 ViT-B/14 Encoder
Self-supervised Vision Transformer pretrained on 142M images (LVD-142M). Extracts rich 768-dimensional visual features without task-specific supervision.
🔍
FAISS Similarity Retrieval
Retrieves the most visually similar training sample in milliseconds using Facebook AI Similarity Search — enabling prototype-based classification.
🧠
Attention Fusion
Scaled dot-product attention fuses query and retrieved support features into a 1536-dim representation for the downstream classifier.
🎲
MC Dropout Uncertainty
40 stochastic forward passes with Dropout active at inference time — giving honest uncertainty estimates alongside every prediction.
📷
Webcam + TTA ×6
Webcam images are auto-preprocessed (contrast, sharpness, brightness) and passed through 6 augmented views — averaged for robust real-world prediction.
📐
ECE Calibration
Expected Calibration Error measured across 2000 training samples with a full reliability diagram — so you know how trustworthy the confidence scores are.
